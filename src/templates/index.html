Frequently Asked Questions

What's Lazy Penguins?
Lazy Penguins is an NFT collection of 10,000 cute & badass penguins algorithmically generated livin' on the Cardano blockchain. Each Penguin comes with its own unique set of traits & accessories inspired from pop culture, memes, personalities and so much more!
How much will the mint cost?
Each Penguin costs 42 ADA.
Will there be a presale/whitelist?
No Presale or Whitelist for early members, we are aiming for a fairer launch as possible. However, those who are early in the community will get a surprise.
When will I get my Penguin after the mint?
Your Penguin will be sent to your wallet right after the minting, which might take few minutes.
Which Cardano wallets can I use?
Use only Yoroi, Daedalus, Nami, or ADAlite wallets for the payments. Do not send ADA from an exchange, otherwise you will not recieve your NFT nor your ADA back.
